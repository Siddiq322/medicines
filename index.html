Medicine Safety Tips

  • Read labels carefully: Check active ingredients, strength, and instructions.
  • Avoid duplicate ingredients: Many cold/flu remedies already contain painkillers like paracetamol.
  • Mind allergies & interactions: Tell your clinician/pharmacist about all medicines and supplements you take.
  • Use the right dose: Dosage depends on age, weight, condition, and formulation. Follow professional advice.
  • Special populations: Pregnancy, breastfeeding, children, elderly, and those with kidney/liver issues need extra caution.
  • Storage: Keep in original packs, away from heat, moisture, and out of children‚Äôs reach.
